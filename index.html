About Us

Hi! My name is Manasi Chaudhary, a freelance graphic designer based in Mumbai. I specialize in digital and print design, editorial design, logo and brand development, and offer designing services to businesses of all sizes.

The best part of being a graphic designer is knowing that I can use my creativity to help others shape their vision and to see my work being used - but THE GREATEST part is that there is always something new to learn, something to get better at. I love the challenge.

My design thinking is rooted in simplicity, which I achieve with user-centric techniques, web standards, and best practices. As Leonardo da Vinci said, “Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.”

Building relationships with clients is something I pride myself on. Working with me means you get a committed design service. One of the many advantages of hiring a freelancer is that you work with one person. I provide personalized attention and guidance throughout the whole process, from the initial concept to the final result, and make sure my clients are true partners in the experience.

I’ve had the opportunity to work on some incredible projects that I’m pleased to share. Feel free to browse through my portfolio. I hope you’ll enjoy viewing my projects as much as I enjoyed working on them. Go ahead and explore, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to learn more.
